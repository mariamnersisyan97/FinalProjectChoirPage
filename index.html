ARENI Choir

As ambassadors of the Armenian culture, our mission is to preserve and promote Armenian and multi-national, classical, ethnic, and contemporary music in diverse communities. By preserving our musical treasures and history, ARENI brings unity and unique cultural value for all generations. To exhibit our heritage, ARENI has insituted the USA's first Dolma Festival and Ghapama Festival, respectively. With its 5th annual Dolma Festival and 3rd Ghapama Festival to come, ARENI has previously presented a Musical Extravaganza in Washington D.C., participated in International Music Festivals in NY, and commemorated the annual and centennial Armenian genocide markings at Times Square. Additionally, ARENI has maintained an annual concert since its emergence at Queens Theatre in the Park, offering new musical presentations with world renowned artists from the Metropolitan Opera. ARENI has recently performed in Philadelphia as well as in Merkin Concert Hall.

Armine Vardanyan (pianist, musical director, conductor)

Ms. Armine Vardanyan matriculated from the Yerevan State Conservatory with a Masters Degree in Piano Performance. Among the professional courses she pursued at the Conservatory, Ms. Vardanyan excelled in theater, singing and conducting chamber choir, the latter becoming her second career. After receiving her Post Graduate Diploma and Ph.D in piano performance, Ms. Vardanyan joined the faculty of the Yerevan State Conservatory. As well as a recording artist, Ms. Vardanyan has been a professor at the Special Music School of New York and at the Lucy Moses School of Music and Art. Ms. Vardanyan's professional performances, include touring throughout the former Soviet Union, Hungary, Cuba and Yugoslavia. In the United States, she has performed at Merkin Hall, Carnegie Hall, Weill Recital Hall, Alice Tully Hall, Pickman Hall in Cambridge, MA, the Grammy Award Ceremony Honoring Mstislav Rostropovich, and Inaugural Ball in Washington DC. She has performed as a soloist with the Armenian State Philharmonic Orchestra. Ms. Vardanyan has had many broadcasts on the National TV of Armenia, as well as in the United States, PBS, FOX 5, NY 1, and French TF 1. She is the recipient of many awards and recognition's. Currently, Ms. Vardanyan is a Professor and director of the piano department at the Long Island Conservatory. She is the Artistic Director of “ARENI Choir” of New York. Ms. Vardanyan has been a judge at the International Competition of the LISMA Foundation for many year as well as the Long Island Conservatory Competition. She was a Member of the Artistic Advisory Committee of the Armenian Youth Talent Competition and has held master classes in numerous international music festivals.
