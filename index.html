ARENI Choir As ambassadors of the Armenian culture, our mission is to preserve and promote Armenian and multi-national, classical, ethnic, and contemporary music in diverse communities. By preserving our musical treasures and history, ARENI brings unity and unique cultural value for all generations. To exhibit our heritage, ARENI has insituted the USA's first Dolma Festival and Ghapama Festival, respectively. With its 5th annual Dolma Festival and 3rd Ghapama Festival to come, ARENI has previously presented a Musical Extravaganza in Washington D.C., participated in International Music Festivals in NY, and commemorated the annual and centennial Armenian genocide markings at Times Square. Additionally, ARENI has maintained an annual concert since its emergence at Queens Theatre in the Park, offering new musical presentations with world renowned artists from the Metropolitan Opera. ARENI has recently performed in Philadelphia as well as in Merkin Concert Hall.
